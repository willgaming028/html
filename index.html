About Us

Healesville Magic Car Wash is a self-serve car wash facility located in Healesville, Victoria. We provide wash bays equipped with high-pressure hoses and cleaning equipment for customers to wash their own vehicles. The facility is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, allowing you to wash your car at your convenience.
